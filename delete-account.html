Foku - Account Deletion Request

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Profile information (display name)
  • Study preferences and settings
  • Study statistics (streaks, focus time, sessions)
  • Chat messages you have sent
  • Notification preferences and push tokens
  • Subscription records
  • Moderation data (reports, blocked users)

Note: Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never stored on our servers, so there is nothing to delete. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through your device's app store before requesting deletion.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to the address below from the email associated with your Foku account.
  2. Include "Delete My Account" in the subject line.
  3. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 3 business days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
