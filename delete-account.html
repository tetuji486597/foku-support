Foku - Account Deletion

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Profile information (display name)
  • Study preferences and settings
  • Study statistics (streaks, focus time, sessions)
  • Chat messages you have sent
  • Notification preferences and push tokens
  • Subscription records
  • Moderation data (reports, blocked users)

Note: Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never stored on our servers. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through your device's app store before deleting your account.

Signed in with Apple? Apple Sign-In is not available on the web. Please use the Delete Account option in the app's Settings to delete your account.
