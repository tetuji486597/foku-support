Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your device's settings. On iOS, go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Foku.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Foku, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

The app isn't detecting when I look away

Make sure you've granted camera permissions to Foku. Go to your device settings and ensure camera access is enabled for the app.

How does the free tier work?

Free users get 60 minutes of focus time per day. Upgrade to Foku Plus for unlimited daily usage, Pomodoro timer, break games, and more!
